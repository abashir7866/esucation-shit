What is cancer?

Right now you may be thinking, what exactly is cancer? Well cancer is known as abnormal cells that mutate or damaged cells that continue to multiply when they shouldnt. In theory it causes cancer in almost anywhere for example but not limited to skin cancer, breast cancer, and many more.

Cell cycle

According to the national human genome research institute the cell cycle is a cycle that properly shows the proccess when cells inevitably replicate. There are different stages that are G1, S, and G2, G1 is the stage when the cell begins to divide. S is the stage when the DNA is copied and theres an entire set of new genetic material for the cell. Most importantly stage G2 begins organizing all of the genetic material including the DNA then finilizing the divide. Lastly the stage M where cell divides into 2 copies which make 2 sister cells.
